Beginner Classic — River Calm Run

Perfect for first-timers and families. Gentle rapids, scenic stretches, and expert instruction make this a relaxed introduction to rafting.

Duration: Half-day (3–4 hrs)
Difficulty: Easy
Price: ₦20,000 / person

What to expect: short safety briefing, 2–3 miles of paddling, riverside picnic spot, optional instructor-led swim/balance practice on calm stretches. Recommended for ages 8+.

Ask about family discounts & availability

Adventure Intermediate — Rapids Run

A full-day trip for paddlers wanting steady thrills: longer rapids, technical lines, and more sustained paddling sections.

Duration: Full-day (6–8 hrs)
Difficulty: Intermediate
Price: ₦45,000 / person (lunch included)

What to expect: in-depth paddling practice, multiple Class II–III rapids, guided scouting of challenging sections, riverside lunch and photography. Good fitness recommended.

Contact us to check trip dates & group rates

Pro Expedition — Rafting Challenge

Designed for experienced paddlers seeking a serious adrenaline day. This run contains sustained, technical rapids and requires prior Rafting experience.

Duration: Full-day / multi-paddle
Difficulty: Advanced
Price: ₦70,000 / person

What to expect: scouting of high-consequence rapids, mandatory safety briefing and rescue plan, guide support, and footage of the run. Participants must be competent paddlers and bring personal paddling gear (we can advise).
